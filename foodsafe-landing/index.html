Process

Three steps to food safety

Powered by Groq AI, FSSAI violation data, and seasonal risk models trained on Indian markets.

01 — SCAN 🔍
Type, Photo or Barcode

Enter any food name in English, Hindi, or Marathi. Or snap a photo of the label. Or scan the barcode directly.

02 — ANALYSE 🧪
AI Cross-References FSSAI

Our AI checks your food against real FSSAI violation records, market adulteration rates, and seasonal risk patterns.

03 — ACT ✅
Get Clear Guidance

Risk score, adulterant list, easy home tests you can do today, safe brands to buy, and personalised warnings for your family.
